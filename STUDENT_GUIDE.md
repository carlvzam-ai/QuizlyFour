# Getting Started: How to Use Quizly Four

## 🎓 Welcome, Students!

Welcome to **Quizly Four** - your open educational resource platform for college minor courses.

## 📱 First Time? Start Here

### Step 1: Explore the Platform
1. Visit the platform and browse the 4 courses
2. Read course descriptions and learning objectives

### Step 2: Choose Your Course
- **Ethics** - Develop moral reasoning
- **The Contemporary World** - Understand global issues
- **Team Sports** - Build teamwork skills
- **Abstracting** - Master information synthesis

### Step 3: Start Learning
Each course has:
- 📖 **Tutorials** - Step-by-step guides
- 📕 **Textbooks** - Study materials
- 🎬 **Videos** - Educational content
- 🧠 **Exercises** - Quizzes and assignments
