## Question 1: Team Definition
**What distinguishes a team from a group?**

A) Teams are larger than groups  
B) Groups work toward a common goal, teams don't  
C) Teams work toward a common goal with coordinated effort  
D) There is no difference  

**Correct Answer:** C

---

## Question 2: Team Roles
**Which role focuses on bringing creative ideas?**

A) The Leader  
B) The Innovator  
C) The Implementer  
D) The Supporter  

**Correct Answer:** B

---

## Question 3: Active Listening
**What is NOT part of active listening?**

A) Looking at the speaker  
B) Planning your response while they talk  
C) Asking clarifying questions  
D) Showing you care  

**Correct Answer:** B

---

## Question 4: Why Teamwork Matters
**What percentage of jobs require teamwork?**

A) 20%  
B) 50%  
C) 80%  
D) Teamwork isn't important in jobs  

**Correct Answer:** C
