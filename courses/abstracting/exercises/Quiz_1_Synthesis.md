## Question 1: Information Synthesis
**What is information synthesis?**

A) Reading one source and summarizing it  
B) Combining information from multiple sources to create new understanding  
C) Copying text from different sources  
D) Ignoring sources you disagree with  

**Correct Answer:** B

---

## Question 2: Synthesis vs. Summary
**What is the key difference between summarizing and synthesizing?**

A) They mean the same thing  
B) Summarizing uses one source; synthesis combines multiple sources  
C) Synthesizing is easier than summarizing  
D) Summaries are longer than syntheses  

**Correct Answer:** B

---

## Question 3: Synthesis Process
**Which is the correct first step in synthesis?**

A) Write immediately  
B) Find connections between sources  
C) Gather and read sources  
D) Organize everything  

**Correct Answer:** C

---

## Question 4: Good Synthesis
**Why should your synthesis include interpretation?**

A) To make it longer  
B) So readers don't need to read original sources  
C) To show your understanding and draw conclusions  
D) To hide what the sources actually say  

**Correct Answer:** C
