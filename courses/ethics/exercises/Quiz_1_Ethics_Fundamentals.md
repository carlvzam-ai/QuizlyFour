## Question 1: Definition of Ethics
**What is ethics?**

A) Rules that only governments create  
B) The study of right and wrong, good and bad conduct  
C) Religious beliefs only  
D) Common sense everyone naturally understands  

**Correct Answer:** B

---

## Question 2: Deontological Ethics
**Which statement best describes deontological ethics?**

A) Actions are right if they produce the best outcome  
B) Some actions are right or wrong in themselves, regardless of outcome  
C) Focus on developing good character traits  
D) Only religious people follow this framework  

**Correct Answer:** B

---

## Question 3: Consequentialist Ethics
**A consequentialist believes:**

A) Follow rules no matter what  
B) The best outcome for the most people matters most  
C) Character is everything  
D) Ethics is too complicated  

**Correct Answer:** B

---

## Question 4: Virtue Ethics
**Virtue ethics emphasizes:**

A) Following the rules  
B) Getting the best outcome  
C) Developing good character traits and virtues  
D) What other people think you should do  

**Correct Answer:** C
