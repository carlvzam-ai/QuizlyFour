# Tutorial 1: Globalization and Global Interconnection

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

By the end of this tutorial, you will:
- Understand what globalization means
- Recognize how countries are interconnected
- Identify positive and negative effects of globalization
- Analyze globalization's impact on your life

---

## 🌍 What is Globalization?

**Globalization** is the process of increased integration and interconnection of countries, economies, cultures, and peoples across the world.

### Simple Definition:
The world is becoming more connected. What happens in one country affects others. People, ideas, money, and products move across borders easily.

---

## 🌐 Three Dimensions of Globalization

### 1. **Economic Globalization**
- Companies operate in multiple countries
- International trade is easier
- Global supply chains

**Example:** Your smartphone made in China, designed in USA, sold worldwide

### 2. **Cultural Globalization**
- Cultures mix and influence each other
- Movies, music, and ideas spread worldwide

**Example:** Watching Korean dramas in the Philippines

### 3. **Political Globalization**
- Countries cooperate more
- International organizations have more power
- Global issues require global solutions

**Example:** United Nations decisions

---

## ✅ Key Takeaways

1. **Globalization is real** - It's reshaping the world daily
2. **It's multi-dimensional** - Economic, cultural, and political aspects
3. **Effects are mixed** - Both benefits and challenges
4. **It's accelerating** - Especially due to technology
5. **You must understand it** - It will define your future
