## Question 1: Definition of Globalization
**What is globalization?**

A) Only the spread of technology worldwide  
B) The process of increased worldwide integration and interconnection  
C) A political movement against international trade  
D) A new type of economy  

**Correct Answer:** B

---

## Question 2: Economic Globalization Impact
**Which is an example of economic globalization?**

A) Watching a foreign movie at home  
B) Your smartphone made in multiple countries and sold worldwide  
C) Learning a foreign language  
D) Traveling to another country  

**Correct Answer:** B

---

## Question 3: Cultural Globalization
**What is a negative effect of cultural globalization?**

A) People learning about different cultures  
B) Exchange of ideas between countries  
C) Loss of traditional cultures and local languages  
D) Increased international friendships  

**Correct Answer:** C

---

## Question 4: Political Globalization
**Political globalization enables:**

A) Individual countries to work completely alone  
B) Countries to cooperate on global problems like climate change  
C) Elimination of all international organizations  
D) Wars to be more frequent  

**Correct Answer:** B
